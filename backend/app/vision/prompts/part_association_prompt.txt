ROLE ASSIGNMENT GUIDELINES:
- **structural**: Base plates, large bricks, frames (load-bearing foundation pieces)
- **decorative**: Colored tiles, printed pieces, stickers (aesthetic elements)
- **functional**: Wheels, hinges, Technic pins, axles (moving/mechanical parts)
- **connector**: Small plates, brackets, clips (joining pieces together)

GUIDELINES:
- Base plates and large bricks → structural
- Wheels, axles, hinges → functional
- Small plates, 1x1 pieces for connecting → connector
- Colored tiles without studs, printed pieces → decorative
- Consider part size, shape, and typical LEGO building patterns
